    What Are Dental Implants and Why Are They Important?

    Dental implants are titanium posts surgically placed into the jawbone to replace missing tooth roots. They provide a strong foundation for fixed or removable replacement teeth, such as crowns, bridges, or dentures. Unlike traditional dentures, implants fuse with the bone, preventing bone loss and maintaining facial structure. Benefits include:

  • Natural appearance and feel

  • Long-lasting durability

  • Improved chewing and speaking ability

  • Preservation of jawbone and facial aesthetics
